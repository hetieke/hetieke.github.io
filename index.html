Biography

I'm Tieke He, I got my Bachelor, Master and Ph.D. degree from Software Institute, Nanjing University in 2010, 2012 and 2017, respectively. My Ph.D. advisor is professor Bin Luo and professor Zhenyu Chen. Currently, I'm working as a research assistant at Software Institute, Nanjing University.

Research Interest

Software Engineering; Crowdsourcing; Knowledge Graph and its Derivative Applications
